
# 🎬 Netflix Content Analytics Dashboard

> **An end-to-end Business Intelligence project built with Power BI to analyze Netflix's content library and uncover actionable business insights.**

---

# 📌 Executive Summary

Netflix has rapidly expanded its content catalog over the past decade. This dashboard analyzes movies and TV shows to understand growth patterns, audience preferences, content quality, and engagement using IMDb metrics.

The report helps answer questions such as:

- How has Netflix's catalog evolved over time?
- Are Movies or TV Shows driving growth?
- Does content quality remain stable as the library expands?
- Which audience certifications dominate the platform?
- Which titles receive the strongest audience response?

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze the growth of Netflix content over time.
- Compare Movies and TV Shows.
- Monitor runtime, IMDb score, and votes.
- Understand audience age certifications.
- Support content acquisition and production decisions.

# 📈 Key Business Insights

## 1. Movies dominate the catalog
Movies significantly outnumber TV Shows, reflecting Netflix's strong investment in feature-length content.

## 2. Content production accelerated after 2015
A steep increase in yearly releases indicates aggressive expansion through originals and licensed content.

## 3. Runtime remains consistent
Average runtime has remained relatively stable, showing standardized production characteristics.

## 4. IMDb quality is stable
Despite rapid catalog growth, average IMDb scores remain fairly consistent.

## 5. Adult audience focus
TV-MA and R certifications represent a large portion of the catalog, suggesting a primary focus on mature audiences.

## 6. Viewer engagement increased
IMDb votes trend upward over time, indicating higher audience participation and platform reach.

## 7. Critically acclaimed classics remain valuable
Top-rated titles continue to attract attention alongside newer releases.
