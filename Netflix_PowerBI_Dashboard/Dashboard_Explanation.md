
# Dashboard Explanation

## Dashboard Purpose

The dashboard provides an interactive overview of Netflix Movies and TV Shows, allowing users to explore content trends, ratings, runtime, and audience engagement.

---

# Dashboard Components

## KPI Cards

### Total Movies
Displays the total number of Movies available in the dataset.

### Total TV Shows
Displays the total number of TV Shows available in the dataset.

---

## Release Year Filter

Allows users to analyze the dashboard for a selected release year.

Business Value:
- Compare different years.
- Analyze growth trends.

---

## Runtime Trend

Shows how average runtime has changed over time.

Business Question:
Has Netflix changed the average duration of its content over the years?

---

## Content Released by Year

Displays the number of Movies and TV Shows released each year.

Business Question:
How quickly has Netflix expanded its content library?

---

## IMDb Score Trend

Shows the average IMDb score across release years.

Business Question:
Has content quality improved or declined over time?

---

## IMDb Votes Trend

Displays audience engagement using IMDb votes.

Business Question:
Which periods generated the highest audience interaction?

---

## Top Rated Titles

Highlights the highest-rated Movies and TV Shows.

Business Value:
Identify benchmark content for future investments.

---

## Age Certification Distribution

Displays the distribution of content across age certifications.

Business Question:
Which audience segment does Netflix primarily target?

---

# Overall Business Value

This dashboard enables business users to monitor catalog growth, understand audience preferences, evaluate content quality, and support strategic decision-making using interactive visualizations.
